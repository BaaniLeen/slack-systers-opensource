Join Systers OSS Projects

Systers Open Source Software (OSS) community is for all those who want to join the discussion and/or contribute to Systers open source projects. We welcome UI/UX designers, developers, testers, and beyond to participate in creating social impact products to help Systers and Peace Corps communities.

We talk about technology, design, implementation, testing methodologies and more to help build products that are innovative and userfull to those who are using them.
